# Eterno Strategy Open Questions

## 2026-03-31 (from strategy meeting)

- **Upstairs plan:** What is the upstairs space for? Gallery, education hub, reading room, hybrid? Needs a concrete plan with use cases, layout, and cost.
- **Incremental steps:** What can be done now vs what requires fundraising/major investment? Need a phased roadmap rather than one big transformation.
- **Platform examples:** Which other platforms/spaces are doing art + technology experience well? Research needed for reference cases, business models, and lessons.
- **Hardware partnerships:** Can we get partnerships for the physical setup? Specific needs: coffee machine, books, sofas, chairs, tables. Who are the right partners and what's the pitch?
- **Bookstore gap in Lisbon:** Is there an underserved niche for art/technology/culture books in Lisbon? Who are the existing players and where is the gap Eterno could fill?
