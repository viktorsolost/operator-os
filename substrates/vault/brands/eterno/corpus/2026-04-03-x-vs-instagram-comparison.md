## Read

Instagram is the stronger discovery surface by a wide margin in this period.
It also gives clearer account-level action data.

X is still useful, but the channel mix matters.
Only 7 of the 17 X items in the matched window were original posts.
The rest were retweets or replies.
So the effective original-post cadence is lower than the headline posting rate.

## Working interpretation

- Instagram is doing more top-of-funnel work.
- X is being used more as a sequencing and amplification layer around launches.
- Eterno's X output in this period is partly dependent on retweeting partner or artist content.
- Instagram has stronger discovery evidence, but conversion is still weak there too because profile activity and link taps are down.

## What should happen next

- separate X original posts from retweets in future scorecards
- connect both channels to site traffic and product-page activity
- treat channel success as reach -> action -> sale, not reach alone
