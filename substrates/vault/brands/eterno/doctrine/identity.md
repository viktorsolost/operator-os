# Eterno Identity

Status: draft

## Working position

Eterno is not treated here as a finished brand.
It is a brand in transition.

Current internal direction points toward Eterno as an art and technology experience in Lisbon, with a stronger digital-native identity, physical-first execution, and digital amplification.

## Important boundary

Nothing in this file should be read as ideal-state truth.
This file is a working synthesis of current evidence.
Every major claim should be challenged against:
- internal strategy notes
- live channel output
- public-facing materials
- commercial performance

## Stable enough to use

- Eterno is moving away from generic gallery/NFT framing.
- Eterno is moving toward an art + technology cultural position.
- The upstairs concept is part of brand identity, not just space planning.
- Physical experience matters, but online strategy and distribution are still weak.

## Not stable enough to lock

- Final positioning language
- Upstairs program mix
- Community model
- Ticketing model
- Exact role of commerce vs culture vs education

## Improvement standard

The doctrine must preserve room for iteration.
The system should surface where Eterno is weak, inconsistent, outdated, or unproven.
