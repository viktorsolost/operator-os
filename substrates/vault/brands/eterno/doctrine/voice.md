# Eterno Voice

Status: draft

## Current reading

Eterno's voice should be treated as an evolving hypothesis, not a solved identity.

Recent internal direction suggests:
- more cultural confidence
- more digital-native specificity
- less generic art marketing language
- less explanatory overload
- more curiosity-driven framing
- more proof, fewer vague claims

## What to test

- Does the writing sound like a serious cultural actor, not a generic gallery?
- Does it feel contemporary without sounding trend-chasing?
- Does it create curiosity rather than over-explaining?
- Does it preserve authority without becoming cold or academic?

## Anti-patterns to track

- generic luxury-gallery language
- shallow tech buzzwords
- NFT-first framing detached from experience
- informative but non-converting copy
- beautiful language with no action path
