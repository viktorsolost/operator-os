# Eterno Instagram Channel

Status: draft

Linked source: [[brands/eterno/sources/instagram/2026-04-03-instagram-insights-screenshot-batch-001]]

## Current read

Instagram is currently the clearer top-of-funnel discovery surface for Eterno than the data we have from X.
A majority of views come from non-followers, which suggests real discovery rather than only existing audience recycling.

## What looks true from current evidence

- Discovery is working better than conversion.
- Posts drive the largest share of views.
- Reels drive the largest share of interactions.
- Instagram is producing audience growth, but profile activity is falling.

## Numbers that matter

- Views: 28,401
- Accounts reached: 9,922
- Interactions: 913
- Followers: 5,803
- Net growth: 54
- Profile visits: 607
- External link taps: 51

## Interpretation

The account is reaching beyond the current follower base.
But profile visits, link taps, and address taps are all down materially.
That means awareness is not translating into downstream action strongly enough.

## Working hypotheses

- Reels should be treated as the stronger interaction format.
- Posts should be treated as the stronger reach format.
- The channel likely needs sharper conversion design once attention is won.
- Instagram success should not be judged by views alone.

## Questions to answer next

- Which exact creative formats produce both reach and profile action?
- Which posts or reels drive link taps, not just views?
- How much of current output is exhibition announcement vs stronger narrative or proof-based content?
- Which Instagram content types correlate with site sessions and sales?
