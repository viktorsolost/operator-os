# Eterno Tensions

Status: draft

## Active tensions

1. Physical-first experience vs digital amplification
2. Cultural depth vs sales execution weakness
3. Strong ambition vs weak measurement discipline
4. Internal strategic clarity vs inconsistent public presentation
5. High-taste positioning vs underdeveloped online conversion system

## Operational tension

Eterno appears to have more strategic thought than execution discipline.
That gap should stay visible in doctrine.
The system should not flatten aspiration into achievement.

## Channel tension

The website shows what exists publicly.
Internal strategy files show what Eterno wants to become.
Those are not the same thing.
